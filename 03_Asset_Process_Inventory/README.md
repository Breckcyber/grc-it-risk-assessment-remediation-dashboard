# Deliverable 3: Asset and Process Inventory

This folder contains the Asset and Process Inventory developed for MapleCare Health Services.

The inventory identifies the systems and business processes included in the technology risk assessment.

For each asset or process, the inventory includes:

* Asset or process name
* Business purpose
* System or process owner
* Data type
* Data sensitivity
* Business criticality
* Key dependencies
* Main technology and cybersecurity concerns
* Initial risk level

The inventory was used to identify which systems and processes required deeper risk assessment and control review.
