# Deliverable 4: Risk Register

This folder contains the Technology Risk Register developed for MapleCare Health Services.

The register documents 15 technology, cybersecurity, privacy, third-party, resilience, and governance risks identified during the assessment.

For each risk, the register includes:

* Risk ID
* Asset or business process
* Risk category
* Risk description
* Business impact
* Likelihood and impact ratings
* Overall risk rating
* Existing controls
* Control gaps
* Risk owner
* Treatment decision
* Management action
* Target date
* Status

The Risk Register was used as the foundation for the Risk and Control Matrix and the subsequent control-testing activities.
