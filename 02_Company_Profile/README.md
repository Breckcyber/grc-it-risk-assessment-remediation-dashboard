# Deliverable 2: Business and Technology Profile

This folder contains the Business and Technology Profile developed for MapleCare Health Services.

The profile documents the organization’s operating environment, technology systems, sensitive information, business processes, key dependencies, and initial technology risk concerns.

The document provides the business context used to support the Asset and Process Inventory, Technology Risk Register, control assessment, and subsequent control-testing activities.
